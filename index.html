Organic Farming Best Practices

  • Crop Rotation: Intercrop legumes (lentils, moong) between cereal crops to fix nitrogen naturally.
  • Mulching: Spread dry leaves/wheat straw around plants to preserve moisture, prevent weed growth, and feed soil microbes.
  • Trap Crops: Sow 1 row of marigold flowers for every 10 rows of cotton or vegetables to trap aphids and nematodes away from the primary crop.
